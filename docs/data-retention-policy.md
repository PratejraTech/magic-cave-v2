# Data Retention Policy
## Family Advent Calendar Application

### Purpose
This policy outlines how long different types of user data are retained in the Family Advent Calendar application, in compliance with GDPR, COPPA, and other applicable privacy regulations.

### Data Retention Periods

#### User Account Data
- **Parent Account Information**: Retained indefinitely until account deletion request
- **Child Profile Data**: Retained until child turns 18 or account deletion request
- **Authentication Data**: Retained for 7 years for security audit purposes

#### Calendar and Content Data
- **Calendar Templates**: Retained indefinitely (non-personal data)
- **User-Generated Calendar Content**: Retained until account deletion
- **Tile Content and Media**: Retained until account deletion
- **Gift Assignments**: Retained until account deletion

#### Analytics and Usage Data
- **Application Analytics**: Retained for 2 years for product improvement
- **Security Events**: Retained for 7 years for compliance and audit purposes
- **Error Logs**: Retained for 90 days

#### Chat and Communication Data
- **Chat History with AI**: Retained for 1 year, then anonymized
- **User Notes and Messages**: Retained until account deletion

### Data Deletion Process

#### User-Initiated Deletion
1. Users can request complete data deletion through the account settings
2. All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
3. Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for product improvement

#### Automatic Deletion
- Inactive accounts (no login for 2 years) are flagged for deletion
- Users receive notification 30 days before automatic deletion
- Child accounts without parent activity for 1 year are reviewed

#### Legal Retention
- Data required for legal compliance is retained as mandated by law
- Security incident data is retained for 7 years

### Data Minimization
- Only collect data necessary for application functionality
- Regularly review and minimize data collection
- Implement data anonymization where possible

### Contact Information
For questions about data retention or deletion requests, contact: privacy@familyadventcalendar.com

### Policy Updates
This policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes.
