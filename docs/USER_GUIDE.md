## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the App**: Go to [Magic Cave Calendars](https://magiccavecalendars.com)
2. **Sign Up**: Click "Get Started" or "Create Account"
3. **Choose Auth Method**:
   - Email & Password
   - Sign in with Google
   - Sign in with Facebook

4. **Enter Your Details**:
   - Your name (parent/guardian)
   - Email address
   - Create a password (if using email auth)

5. **Add Child Profile**:
   - Child's name
   - Birthdate
   - Gender (optional, helps personalize content)
   - Interests (select from: butterflies, dogs, cats, horses, swings, books, art, music)

6. **Choose a Theme**:
   - Winter Wonderland (snowy, peaceful)
   - Candy Cane Lane (festive, colorful)
   - Northern Lights (magical, ethereal)
   - Cozy Christmas (warm, traditional)

7. **Complete Setup**: Your calendar is created with 25 empty tiles!
