Frequently asked

How do I invite another adult?

Visit the Account page and add a collaborator. They’ll receive an email + optional SMS link.

Can I reuse last year’s tiles?

Yes—duplicate a calendar from the dashboard, then edit dates, gifts, or copy.

How are kids protected?

Child mode is read-only, requires PIN access, and hides adult comments or drafts.
